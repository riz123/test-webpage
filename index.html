About ShilpAstra

Built on craft.
Delivered with precision.

"Shilp" is the art of building with mastery; "Astra" is the instrument that makes it possible. Together, they describe how we work — combining disciplined engineering with hands-on craftsmanship on every site we touch.

ShilpAstra Infratech Pvt Ltd is a construction and infrastructure development company delivering roads, residential townships, industrial facilities and commercial complexes across India. Since our founding, we've built a reputation for taking on technically demanding projects and executing them on schedule, without compromising on quality.

Our in-house teams cover planning, structural engineering, site execution and quality control, which means fewer handoffs, faster decisions, and a single point of accountability from groundbreaking to handover.

We work with public sector bodies, private developers and industrial clients, and we hold ourselves to the same standard on every job: build it right, build it on time, and build something that outlasts the contract.
